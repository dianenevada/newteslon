About us

Like many substantial establishments, ours has a story too. Teslon was founded on the 4th of March 2023 by a group of young engineers undergoing internship at Fundi Bots.

The startup was inspired by the great Nikola Tesla, a Serbian-American inventor, electrical engineer, mechanical engineer, and futurist best known for his contributions to the design of the modern alternating current (AC) electricity supply system.
